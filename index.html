Welcome

I am currently a National Science Foundation Postdoctoral fellow working at Cornell University and the Materials Process Design and Control Laboratory. My work is focused on researching the application of finite element methods to model the micro-structure evolution of snow. The resulting model will assist in gaining further understanding the complex nature of snow metamorphism, which will aid in the prediction of weak-layer formation within the snowpack.
